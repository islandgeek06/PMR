About Us

Getting your songs played as an independent artist is tough. There's so much music being generated these days, it's hard to stand out. At the same time, DJs hate being bombarded by artists they've never heard of, to play music they haven't vetted during their sets. With all the technology available today, we knew there had to be a better way!

Our founders have over two decades of professional, international DJ experience.
